Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services and facilities.

What amenities are nearby?
There are numerous restaurants, massage shops, and local attractions just up the hill. Our island map guide can help you explore further.
Is the pool shared?
Yes, the pool is shared among seven units, including four seaview and three non-seaview accommodations. The pool is rarely crowded as most guests are often exploring Phuket.
What transport options are available?
For transport, we recommend booking in advance with our neighbor, Mr. A which has a network of trusted licenced taxi drivers. Popular ride-hailing apps like Grab, Bolt, and InDrive are also available. You can also rent motorbikes with Mr.A which lives 20 meters from the villa
What is the check-in time?
Our check-in time starts at 2:00 PM. If you plan to come earlier when the room is ready, we can store your bags while you enjoy the pool
Do you offer airport transportation?
Yes, we offer airport transportation with our trusted neighbor Mr.A, Please contact us with your flight details and we will arrange for you, it costs 900 baht.
Are pets allowed in the villa?
Unfortunately, we do not allow pets in the villa to ensure comfort and safety for all our guests.
How often is cleaning provided?
We offer one free cleaning per week. Additional cleanings are available for 400 baht per session.
Do you provide beach towels?
We do not offer beach towels, but we provide Thai-style beach mats and yoga mats for guest use.
Is there a quiet time policy?
Yes, quiet hours are from 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM. While the lights turn off at 10:00 PM, you are welcome to swim anytime as long as you respect quiet hours.
Is there a laundry service available?
We do not offer in-house laundry services. However, there are several local laundry facilities nearby that offer quick service by the kilo.
Is the beach safe for swimming?
Yes, the beach is safe year-round as it is protected by the bay. During low tide, we recommend swimming about 200 meters to the left to avoid rocky sand beds.
Can we drink the tap water?
Tap water is not drinkable but is safe for cooking. Bottled water is readily available at 7-Eleven and Tops Daily minimarts nearby.
